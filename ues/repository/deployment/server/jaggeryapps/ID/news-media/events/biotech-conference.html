Biotechnology is a global industry that leverages the power of science, technology and innovation and business to develop innovative solutions to address some of the daunting challenges of the 21 century. Therefore it is timely to call upon to a national platform to share and pool the resources and to vision the way forward in a coordinated manner to achieve the goal of increasing the hi-tech exports from 1.5% to 10% as stated in the National ST&I strategy for Sri Lanka. A National Conference and Exhibition, is now planned to promote a dialogue amongst the R&D organizations and industry to encourage collaboration for enhanced outcomes around topics of mutual interests to energize the national strategies.
